Welcome to the Realm of Smart Home Pricing

This project aims to predict house prices using machine learning techniques, focusing on the Ames Housing Dataset. Through rigorous data preprocessing, feature engineering, and modeling, the study utilizes XGBoost, an efficient regression model for tabular data. The analysis demonstrates the influence of key features like living area, overall quality, and total square footage on house prices. The real estate market is dynamic, influenced by various factors like size, location, and amenities. Accurate prediction of house prices is crucial for stakeholders, including buyers, sellers, and policymakers. This study employs advanced machine learning techniques to address the challenges of price prediction, showcasing a comprehensive pipeline from data collection and preprocessing to modeling and evaluation. By leveraging the Ames Housing Dataset, this work highlights the effectiveness of feature engineering and modern regression methods, providing a robust framework for price estimation.

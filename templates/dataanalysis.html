Important Fields and Data Ranges

OverallQual (Overall Quality)

Rates the overall material and finish of the house on a scale from 1 to 10.

Input Range: 1 to 10

GrLivArea (Ground Living Area)

Total above-ground living area in square feet.

Input Range: 334 to 5642 sq ft

Common Range: 1129.5 to 1776.75 sq ft

GarageCars (Number of Cars in Garage)

Size of the garage in terms of car capacity.

Input Range: 0 to 4 cars

Common Range: 1 to 2 cars

TotalSF (Total Square Footage)

Total area including first floor, second floor, and basement.

Input Range: 334 to 11,752 sq ft

Common Range: 2009.5 to 3004 sq ft

YearBuilt (Year Built)

Year the house was originally constructed.

Input Range: 1872 to 2010

Common Range: Houses are generally built between 1954 and 2000

Example Use Case

Scenario

A buyer considers two houses with these specifications:

House A

  • Living Area: 2000 sq ft
  • Garage Capacity: 2 cars
  • Total Area: 2500 sq ft
  • Year Built: 2005

House B

  • Overall Quality: 5
  • Living Area: 1500 sq ft
  • Garage Capacity: 1 car
  • Total Area: 1800 sq ft
  • Year Built: 1980

Predictions (these are static values)

  • House A: $320,000
  • House B: $250,000

Project Description

My inspiration for this project is the challenges faced by many elderly individuals or those with memory loss. To address these challenges, I created an app that helps users take note of important details, such as with whom they talk and their conversations. The app includes several functionalities to make their lives easier:

  • Conversation History: Logs all interactions, including the people they talked to and their conversations.
  • Location Tracking: Records their location history to help them recall activities.
  • Categorized Contacts: Tags contacts (e.g., family, friends, colleagues) for better organization.
  • Last Conversation Tracker: Provides quick access to the last conversation with each individual for continuity.
